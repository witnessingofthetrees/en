witnessing of the trees is a performative promenade through the garden of the former Hospedaria de Imigrantes.

you will encounter 6 trees talking to you.

the tour lasts about 34 minutes, with 7 stops in the garden of Museu da Imigração.

Swipe to read more

Your experience will be more intense if you use your headphones. Ask someone from the museum for a leaflet with a map. Follow the stops according to the numbers.

Press the menu icon at the top right of the screen to change stops.

Use the buttons at the bottom of the screen to move to the next or previous stop.

This trail starts just after the security check at the entrance of Museu da Imigração.
